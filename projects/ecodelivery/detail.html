Asendia Service - Eco Delivery

This is a service designed for the Asendia Company (logistic and delivery company) it consists on a sustainable shipping solution that ASENDIA will offer to the seller as a new attractive service for online shops, and the buyer will choose it to have a more convenient and sustainable online shop experience.

This service is for online shoppers who feel more satisfied by sustainable shopping in order to give them a happier online shopping experience and the satisfaction of an eco-friendly experience for choosing the sustainable shipping. And for stores and brands that want an unique service to attract more customers in order to have new market opportunities and less expenses on the deliveries.

The user experience consist on:

1. The seller add Asendia plug-in at their website, in order to offer this sustainable service.

2. The buyer find a product he/she likes on the social media and search for it online.

3. During the research, the user find it on different online platforms, but choose this particular website to feel more eco-friendly.

4. The seller receive the order from the buyer, go to Asendia website in order to book a pick-up appointment and prepare the products for the shipping.

5. Asendia receive the booking request for the pick-up from the seller, open a new shipping procedure for a determined area and while waiting that the order is getting full, pick-up the items from the buyers.

6. Asendia pack the product for the shipping, with the sustainable shopper certificate.

7. Automatically upload the tracking information to the seller's website where the buyer and the seller can check it.

8. When the truck is at 80% full, or in one week, the truck depertures and then, the buyer receives the certificate of sustainable shopper.

This project was developed in collaboration with Marta Angelini and Dipti Ganeriwala
