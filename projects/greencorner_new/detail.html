Green Corner


Parting from the importance of the stores that sell products 100% natural and in collaboration with the industrial designer Aranza was developed this concept, where the design is simple and functional.

The materials were selected to be natural or ecologist so we reduce the pollution. The two main materials were concrete, that will least for a long time on good conditions, so we reduce the contamination of changing it often, and wood, natural element took from the environment.
