Monsterify

Monsterify is a children storytelling interactive game for kids from 5 to 8 years old that develop creativity, guide them through the critical decision process and help them to tell stories in order to improve their communication and collaboration for their social development.

Based on augmented reality, it encourage kids to explore the environment, discover new monsters, interact with them and create every moment a new story in the city, share it and start a story telling process that allows them to communicate.

The Profile section will allow the users to see their achievements and their stories.

There are prizes for different achievements, like creating a creative story or making identifying faces on the building and creating the story based on that, for making collaborative stories, or for exploring new areas, etc.

The community section will show the list of friends and teams that the user has created.

Security is an important topic (mostly for the age of the user), so adding friends is an action can only be done in person where parents will see who they are adding. It will close area, the ones that want to become new friends will need to shake their phone at the same time and the app will find them using bluetooth.

The feed section is where all the new stories will appear showing who made them and with the swipe option down the user can see all of them.

By touching one story, they will be on whole screen in order to play them.
